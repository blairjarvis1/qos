What is this experience?

Set among emerald rice paddies and jungle in Gianyar, Bali, the Elevation Barn is a place to arrive fully — and leave changed.

Seven days of restorative yoga, guided meditation, and embodied movement — held alongside extraordinary farm-to-table meals, communal evenings, and the unhurried rhythm of Balinese life. This is not a programme about optimising yourself. It's about returning to yourself.

Led by internationally-trained facilitators with backgrounds in trauma-informed yoga, somatic movement, and nature therapy. Suitable for all experience levels. The maximum group size of 12 guests ensures a genuinely intimate experience where connection forms naturally.

Facilities & space

Every element of the Elevation Barn is designed to deepen your retreat — not distract from it.

Open-air yoga shala
Purpose-built, rice paddy-facing yoga shala with teak floors and all equipment provided.
Infinity pool

Jungle trails

Food & nourishment

Three meals a day, plus snacks, herbal teas, and cold-pressed juices throughout. Every meal is a considered act.

The kitchen at Elevation Barn is plant-forward, locally sourced, and prepared with extraordinary care. Inspired by Balinese tradition and guided by seasonal availability, meals are designed to nourish the body and delight the senses — never to restrict.

Some fish and ethically sourced chicken are available. All dietary requirements are accommodated with advance notice.
