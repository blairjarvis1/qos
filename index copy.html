Set in a restored 18th-century farmhouse in the golden hills of Alentejo, Quinta da Levada is a place to slow down, go inward, and return to yourself.

Seven days of restorative yoga, guided forest bathing, and somatic movement — held alongside long farm-to-table lunches, evenings under the stars, and the deep, unhurried quiet of the Portuguese countryside. This is not a programme about doing more. It's about arriving, fully, at rest.

Led by internationally-trained facilitators with backgrounds in trauma-informed yoga, nature therapy, and integrative coaching. Suitable for all experience levels. Maximum 12 guests ensures a genuinely intimate experience.

Your week at Quinta da Levada

A gentle rhythm, held by nature and each other.

Day 1
Arrival & Settling
Transfer from Lisbon, welcome lunch, orientation walk and opening circle. Early evening yin yoga to decompress from travel. Communal dinner.
Days 2–3
Deepening
Morning movement (vinyasa or restorative), guided meditation, afternoon forest bathing, workshops on breath and somatic awareness. Free time for rest or journalling.
Days 4–5
Stillness & Integration
Morning silence practice, embodied movement, afternoon free time for pool, walking trails or 1:1 sessions. Evening sharing circles.
Days 6–7
Closing & Return
Integration practices, letter-writing workshop, closing ceremony and farewell dinner. Morning 7: departure after a final breakfast, return transfer to Lisbon.
